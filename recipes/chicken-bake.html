Chicken Bake

Description

Try a new food!

Its like the hotpocket of your dreams!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Chicken
  • Bacon Crisp
  • Cheese
  • Ranch Dressing
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Roll out the pizza dough and slice it into even pieces.
  2. Spread the dressing over the dough.
  3. Top it off with chicken and bacon.
  4. Add a handful of mozzerlla cheese, roll it up and sprinkle on some parmesan.
  5. Bake at 415 degrees F until golden brown
