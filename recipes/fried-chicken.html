Fried Chicken

Description

Nothing beats home cooked fried chicken!

As good as your mama made it!

Ingredients

  • 3 Large boneless, skinless breasts (Thinned and separated into slices)
  • 4 Legs
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/2 panko
  • 15 crushed saltines
  • 1/2 tablespoon of salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tablespoon chicken rub
  • 1 tablespoon seasoned salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Salt the chicken
  2. Coat in plain flour
  3. Dip in egg + milk mixture
  4. Coat in breading
  5. Then repeat
